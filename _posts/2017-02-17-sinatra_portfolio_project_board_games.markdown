I love board games! I have loved since I was a little kid since my dad taught me how to play checkers. 

Awesomely, my board game collection has gotten too huge... I have lost track of I have so I decided to build an app that keeps track of my board games for me!

In my app, you can sign up and login in and add/delete/edit the games you have.

I intend to release this on heroku and use it within my board game group (there are about 100 people) so we can see who has the games they want to play!
